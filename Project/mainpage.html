    GOT QUESTIONS? WE GOT
    ANSWERS.

    What time do I need to order my product to ship out within the same week?

    To help ensure your order arrives the same week it was placed, you'll need to place your order by 2pm ET on Wednesday.

    Where do you ship and when can I expect my order?

    We can ship to the 48 contiguous United States, plus Washington D.C. Most packages will ship Monday - Wednesday, and be sent via UPS 2-day shipping (actual delivery times may vary).

    Is your packaging recyclable?

    Yes, the shipping box and insulation are curbside recyclable. Recycling guidelines do vary by location, so please check individual packaging and local guidelines for more information.

    HOW MUCH STORAGE SPACE DO I NEED IN MY FREEZER?

    Storage space varies based on your order; however, we recommend having at least 11″ x 7″ x 6″ of space in your freezer to store the products.

    I AM NOT RECEIVING EMAILS ABOUT MY ORDER. WHO DO I CONTACT?

    Emails may be going to your spam folder. Please check your spam folder, and if you still do not see any emails, please contact us at ethanandteam@beyondmeat.com

    WHAT ARE YOUR PRODUCTS MADE OF?

    Protein, fat, minerals, carbohydrates, and water are the five building blocks of meat. We source these building blocks directly from plants, to create delicious, mouthwatering plant-based meat. Some of these ingredients include pea, brown rice, cocoa butter, calcium, iron and beet juice extract. Please visit our product pages for a full list of ingredients.

    WHAT DOES BEYOND MEAT® TASTE LIKE?

    Beyond Meat is designed to have the same taste and texture as animal-based meat, so you can use it in your favorite dishes while feeling good about the choice you’re making for you and the planet. Learn more about all the Beyond Meat products here.

    WHY DO YOU CREATE PLANT-BASED PRODUCTS FROM PLANTS THAT AIM TO REPLICATE MEAT?

    Our brand promise is to enable consumers to Eat What You Love™, and in this case juicy, delicious burgers, beef, sausages and more. At Beyond Meat, we believe there is a better way to feed the planet. By shifting from animal to plant-based meat, we can address four growing global issues: human health, climate change, constraints on natural resources, and animal welfare.
